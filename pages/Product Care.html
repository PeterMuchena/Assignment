SHOE CARE

Taking proper care of your shoes will help them last longer, stay comfortable, and look new. Always clean your shoes regularly by removing dirt with a soft cloth or brush, and use the correct cleaning method depending on the material such as leather, suede, canvas, or sports shoes. Avoid soaking shoes in water and let them dry naturally instead of using direct heat. Store your shoes in a cool, dry place and avoid wearing the same pair every day so they can air out. You can also use protective sprays to prevent damage from water and stains, and keep them fresh by allowing air circulation or using odor control methods. With proper care, your shoes will maintain their quality, appearance, and durability over time,and use protective sprays to guard against water and stains. These simple steps will help keep your shoes in good condition for a long time.

TECH FLEECE CARE

To take care of your track suits, wash them using cold water and mild detergent to prevent damage and fading. Turn them inside out before washing to protect the fabric and color, and avoid using bleach or strong chemicals. It is best to air dry your track suits instead of using high heat, as heat can shrink or weaken the material. Do not iron directly on printed designs, and store them in a clean, dry place

    Here are the steps to follow for taking care of track suits: Turn inside out before washing to protect the fabric and design Wash with cold water using mild detergent Avoid bleach or strong chemicals to prevent damage Use a gentle wash cycle if using a washing machine Do not mix with rough clothes like jeans Air dry instead of using a dryer Avoid high heat when drying or ironing Do not iron directly on prints or logos Store in a clean, dry place Avoid overwashing to maintain quality
